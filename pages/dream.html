Losing Dreams Again and Again

Problem

Dream is important for the young who want to live a productive life. During the period when I had a dream, I felt good in making efforts to achieve it. In the contrary, I usually wasted plenty of time during the period with no dream.

Yesterday night, I suddenly realised an astonishing fact.

I have not achieved even a single dream in the past.

At first I thought it was unbelievable, since It seemed that I was successful enough compared with the majority of people. I got my bachelor's degree in a 985 university, I was recommended to pursue my master's degree in my school, and I got a good job in one of the top 3 Internet companies in China ...

Truth is hidden under the fact. If you compare the accomplishments with my dreams at the corresponding periods of time, you will know what I'm talking about.

  • I dreamed to go to Tianjin University, but I only came to DLUT
  • I dreamed to be recommended to a more famous university, but I only went on studying at my own school
  • I dreamed to go to Microsoft/NetEase Game/Google, but I only got offers from BAT.

I enjoyed the feeling of owning a dream, but I lost one after another. I could adjust to the life shortly after the failure. That made the hurt fade out quickly.

Reason

There are many reasons for it. The most important ones are

  1. I don't want to achieve the dream that much.
  2. I always look for an alternative, and make myself believe it's also a good choice.

As for the first reason, I remember that I have watched a video from Youku, taking about how people can success. The most inspirational words are

The reason why you don't success is that you don't want that much. When you want to succeed as bad as you want to breath, then you will be successful.

As far as the second one is concerned, I think it's due to the fact that I am not confident enough. I usually consider the worst result that I can meet with. I am afraid of losing the game, so I can't just bravely go ahead. I made a lot of excuses, such as there had been no one done that(Google or NetEase Game), or I had wasted too much time before(every vacation), making it sounded reasonable for looking for an alternative.

I think of a story of a girl, who was admitted to Peking University after 2 years' great effort. Before her, no one had been admitted to a first-class university, let alone Peking University.

I usually dreamed of walking near the WeiMing Lake, and studying in the teaching building of Pecking University. At that period of time, from time to time, I thought that, if only one can be admitted to Peking University, why I can't be that one.

She didn't make any excuse and look for any alternatives. She made great sacrifice and effort, and finally achieved her dream.

Solution

I don't want to lose any dreams any more. I want to win next time.

  • Don't show off my little achievement, even before parents
  • Don't find an alternative or excuse, only the dream

Success is more important than sleep, good relationship with friends, contemporary enjoyment, and good look. I want to achieve my dream!
