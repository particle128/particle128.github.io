Headache

2013-11-04

Today I encountered bad headache again. Although I had made up mind to overcome this disaster without the help of any medicine, I failed. After taking two cold capsules, hurt is fading away.
Headache is not a big deal, but it troubles me a lot. So, I'm going to make a list of the cause, how to avoid and what to do if it has already happened.

The Cause:

  • excessive drink
  • afternoon nap not on time
  • have a cold
  • two many [secret]

How to Avoid:

  • Don't drink unless it is a must. Learn to say no when others propose you a toast. Remember that a true hero is not evaluated according to his behavior on the table.
  • Never destroy the routine whatever happens.
  • Hard to avoid.
  • Minimize the frequency of it.

What to do if it has already happened:

  • If it is serious, don't hesitate to take some pills and go to bed for a rest. Don't bite the bullet in this case.
  • If not, just return to normal and watch the situation. When the situation keeps the same or turns bad, take some medicine.
  • Last but not least, don't waste too much time under the circumstance. Although it's uncomfortable, it's not such unbearable.
